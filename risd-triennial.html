RISD Graphic Design Triennial 2024

I lead a small web design team, functioning as both primary designer and sole developer of the 2024 triennial site.

The design translated the print and spatial identity to the web, and was developed conceptually under my lead. The dots on the homepage (links to individual works) randomize location, or "expire," with every refresh to reflect the curatorial direction.
